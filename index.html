How To Beat Minecraft

From Start ----> To Elytra



1. Mine 4 Wood (Any Type)

2. Make Pickaxe Using 3 Planks (Any Type) and 2 Sticks

3. Mine 15 Stone

3. Make Stone Pickaxe, Stone Shovel, Stone Axe, Furnace

4. Get Some Food (Kill Cows,Pigs,Sheeps)

5. Make A Bed (You Will Need 3 Wool And 3 Planks Of Any Type)

6. Get Some Coal And Iron (42 Raw Iron) (32 Coal)

7. Smelt The Iron With The Coal
